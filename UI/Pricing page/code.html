Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Prorated charges will apply automatically.

What is a "Flow"?

A flow is a single visual command chain (e.g., a "Welcome User" command or a "Moderation" filter). Complex logic counts as one flow.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

We don't offer a trial, but we have a generous refund policy. If you're not happy within the first 14 days, contact support for a refund.

What happens if I hit my limit?

Your bots will continue to function, but you won't be able to add new flows or bots until you upgrade or delete existing ones.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes! We love supporting communities. Contact our sales team with proof of your status for a special discount.

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. Payments are securely processed via Stripe.
